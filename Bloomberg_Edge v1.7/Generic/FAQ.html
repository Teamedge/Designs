Are user names and passwords case-sensitive?


Yes. When the upper-case and lower-case pattern of your user name or password do not match what you used when registering, we are unable to verify who you are.
You are then returned a prompt that says: The username and password you entered are not valid. You should try again with the username/password you used during your registration process.


How do I change my magazine subscription information?


If you wish to change the name and address to which your magazine is delivered, using your account number, please log in to Bloomberg Businessweek's customer care center at http://www.businessweek.com/custserv/manage.htm


How do I cancel my subscription?


If you wish to cancel your magazine subscription, using your account number, please log in to Bloomberg Businessweek's customer care center at http://www.businessweek.com/custserv/manage.htm.
